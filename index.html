Matas Pajarskas

artist name:

Pajja

Drawing inspiration from nature and movement, Pajja’s sound invites listeners to explore and reflect. His artistic practice spans both composition/production and DJing, with his latest album ‘altering’ being an exploration of feelings and experiences that lie beyond language or reason. Lithuanian-born, Amsterdam-based, Pajja works to create a liberating atmosphere where listeners can fully immerse themselves in the freedoms of sound. Dedicated to experimentation, his DJ sets seamlessly move between house, techno, trance and breaks, guiding dancers to a space for emotion and expression in movement. Pajja’s ambient sets remain his truest passion, with a focus on relaxing, meditative and obscure sounds, offering space to slow down and drift.
